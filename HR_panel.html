Frequently Asked Questions
How do I invite candidates?
Go to My Assessments → View Results → Invite button
Can I reuse questions?
Yes — use the Question Bank to save and reuse across assessments.
How is auto-grading done?
MCQs are graded automatically. Text answers are flagged for manual review.
Can I export results?
Yes — PDF or Excel export available on the Results page.
