FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I create an exam?

Click "Start Free," choose a template or blank exam, add your questions, then share the secure link with candidates.

Can candidates take exams without login?

Yes. Candidates open the secure link, verify their name and email, and start immediately — no account required.

Does it support coding questions?

Yes. InterviewPro includes a live code editor with multiple languages and automatic test-case evaluation.

Can I export reports?

Yes. Every assessment includes exportable score reports and candidate-level analytics in PDF or CSV.

Is my data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with role-based access controls for your team.
